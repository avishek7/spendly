Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly: your name, email address, and the expense records you create (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions). We do not collect payment information or financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to operate and improve the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts for typography, which may log your IP address when fonts are loaded. Beyond this, we do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app.
